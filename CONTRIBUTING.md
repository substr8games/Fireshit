# Contributing to Fireshit

Fireshit accepts discussion, testing, defect reports, documentation corrections, and proposed code or asset contributions under centralized project authority.

## Before contributing

Read:

- `LICENSE`
- `LEGAL/FIRESHIT_LEGAL_FRAMEWORK.md`
- `LEGAL/FIRESHIT_THIRD_PARTY_POLICY.md`
- `LEGAL/FIRESHIT_AI_PROVENANCE.md`
- `SECURITY.md`

A contribution must follow the project’s architectural, build, packaging, and release rules in addition to these legal rules.

## Ownership gate

Substantive outside contributions require an executed Fireshit Contributor Copyright Assignment before merge.

Opening a pull request does not guarantee acceptance and does not by itself transfer ownership.

Trivial typo fixes and noncopyrightable suggestions may be accepted without an assignment at the discretion of Giz@SUBSTR8Games.

## Contributor declarations

A proposed contribution must disclose:

- the human contributor;
- employer or client ownership concerns;
- all copied or adapted third-party material;
- every applicable third-party license;
- AI assistance used;
- named source material supplied to an AI system;
- generated files;
- new dependencies; and
- any patent concern known to the contributor.

## License intake

Do not submit GPL, AGPL, SSPL, LGPL, MPL, EPL, CDDL, ShareAlike, unknown-license, or otherwise barred material.

Do not paste code from forums, repositories, generated answers, or documentation without proving that it may lawfully enter Fireshit.

Permissive third-party material still requires notice and review.

## Marks and assets

Contribution of code does not grant permission to use Fireshit names, icons, logos, badges, or official-status language outside the accepted Fireshit work.

## Security reports

Do not submit active vulnerabilities through a public contribution channel. Follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Project authority

Giz@SUBSTR8Games may accept, reject, revise, split, postpone, revert, or remove contributions to preserve the suite’s architecture, quality, security, and legal integrity.
