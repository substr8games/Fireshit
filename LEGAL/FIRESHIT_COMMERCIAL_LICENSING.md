# Fireshit Commercial Licensing
## Version 0.1

FCIL is the public license for Fireshit. It prohibits commercial redistribution, paid rehosting, proprietary packaging, and other forms of capture described in the license.

Giz@SUBSTR8Games may grant separate written commercial permission without changing the public license.

## Activities that may require a commercial agreement

Examples include:

- OEM installation;
- proprietary hardware bundling;
- paid redistribution;
- private-label distribution;
- managed deployments;
- hosted or cloud access;
- enterprise products incorporating Fireshit;
- distribution inside a closed operating-system image;
- use of official marks in commercial product identity; and
- support or certification carrying official Fireshit status.

## A request should identify

- the requesting legal person or entity;
- contact information;
- the intended product or service;
- Fireshit versions and modules involved;
- whether source or binaries will be modified;
- distribution method;
- expected users or customers;
- territory;
- expected term;
- marks requested;
- support expectations; and
- any proposed payment or other consideration.

## No implied exception

The following do not grant commercial permission:

- sponsorship;
- donations;
- Patreon membership;
- issue or pull-request discussion;
- an email summary;
- public praise;
- prior cooperation;
- payment made without an executed agreement; or
- silence from the project.

Permission exists only in a signed written agreement identifying the permitted activity.

## Typical agreement subjects

A commercial agreement may address:

- covered versions and modules;
- permitted products and channels;
- fees;
- term and territory;
- modification rights;
- source access;
- security obligations;
- support;
- marks;
- audit or reporting;
- transfer and sublicensing;
- warranties;
- indemnity;
- termination; and
- governing law.

A commercial agreement applies only to its parties. It does not weaken FCIL for the public or other recipients.
