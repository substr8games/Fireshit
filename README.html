Fireshit: an independently handcrafted set of tools for Wayfire Compositor via Arch Linux.

May or may not work on other distros.

A modular overhaul of the Wayfire UX that enables seamless passive and active monitoring, zero-copy/almost no latency screen capture, and almost universal settings accessibility across your entire neglected userspace.

Comes as a suite including: SUBSTR8 HUD, Firemod, and [frametap].

        

  • 3 terminals in a passthrough overlay that can be selected with SUPER + Home
  • Provides a soothing, flashing interface that delivers telemetry at a glance
  • Stays above your desktop and behind any open windows by default
  • Press SUPER+Home to change the active state: desktop→overlay→focused→none
  • Includes settings for: htop, btop, iotop, nvtop, nethogs and opensnitchd
  • Monitors the TTY running Wayfire in a dedicated panel through a small VM

  • Captures an application window, an entire screen, or a custom region
  • Creates a live shareable viewport, an .mkv recording, or both
  • Launch apps directly with frametap [options] -- <application> [args...]
  • Records only the launched application’s audio while leaving microphones and other apps untouched (when targeting an application)
  • Records global audio output when recording a region or fullscreen
  • Selects capture targets through focused-window, application-ID, view-ID, full-screen, or region modes
  • Uses zero-copy logic to pipe pixels directly from metal, through multiplex and straight to disk with minimal added latency
  • Also takes screenshots in fullscreen and snipping mode

Firemod acts as the central brain of the suite, tracking your tools and scanning your system configuration behind the scenes without imposing heavy background overhead.

  • Unified Module Registry: Coordinates tool execution by dispatching global actions to registered first-party and external capabilities.
  • Curated Settings Discovery: Scans system paths for recognized configuration formats (INI, TOML, JSON, YAML) while filtering out orphaned or duplicate files.
  • Draft & Revision Safety: Manages a secure, local settings draft workspace while automatically preserving up to ten historical recovery states.

It projects your internal toolchain directly to your cursor tip, utilizing a highly optimized, pointer-centered overlay designed for instant tactical execution.

  • Flat-Top Hex Grid: Spawns a spatial honeycomb utility grid at your mouse tip that maps system actions to fixed, muscle-memory positions.
  • Unifying Super Bind: Uses a lightning-fast SUPER+middle-click pointer hook to trigger overlay summoning, relocation, or dismissal.
  • Integrated Color Picker: Houses a local 9x9 magnifying crosshair to sample pixels and copy multiple raw format values directly to your clipboard.
